About Blake Consultancy

As a senior global marketing communications thought-leader, Euan Blake is a specialist in delivering award-winning strategies and campaigns within the luxury hospitality sector. Alongside decades of B2C experience, he has also delivered high-impact strategic B2B and corporate guidance across all media channels worldwide.

Previous employers and agency clientele range broadly across hotel and resort operators, developers and construction firms, advisory services and retail asset managers. With more than 25 years of international leadership experience, the practice offers board-level strategic communications expertise and multi-territory campaign execution across EMEA, APAC, the Americas and the Indian Ocean.

The consultancy integrates earned, owned and paid media, social, influencer, content and live event activations with global brand positioning and narrative creation, always anchored in commercial outcomes.

Approach

The consultancy philosophy is built on strategic clarity, simple and actionable frameworks and performance-driven, measurable communications.

Campaigns are orchestrated across multiple cultural and market landscapes, with premium storytelling that connects asset-level USPs, guest experience and investor priorities.

AI-enabled media monitoring, sentiment analysis and ROI dashboards support transparent reporting and value tracking.
